Return & Refund Policy
Last updated: July 2026

Eligible Returns

Most products may be returned if they are defective, damaged, incorrect, or significantly different from the description. Return requests should normally be submitted within the stated return period after delivery.

Non-returnable Items

  • Products damaged by misuse.
  • Personal care items that have been opened.
  • Customized or personalized products.
  • Digital products and downloadable content.
  • Items marked as non-returnable at purchase.

Refund Process

Once the returned item has been inspected and approved, refunds will be processed using the original payment method whenever possible. If this is not possible, another approved refund method may be offered.

Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days after the returned product has been received and approved. Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

Seller Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate product descriptions.
  • Ship products in good condition.
  • Respond promptly to return requests.
  • Honor approved refunds according to marketplace policy.

Customer Responsibilities

  • Inspect products upon delivery.
  • Submit return requests within the allowed period.
  • Return items with all accessories and original packaging where applicable.
  • Provide accurate information during the return process.
