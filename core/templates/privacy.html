Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, placing an order, contacting customer support, or registering as a seller. This may include your name, email address, phone number, delivery address and payment-related information.

How We Use Information

Your information helps us process orders, improve marketplace security, communicate with you, provide customer support, prevent fraud and improve your shopping experience. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Payment Security

Payments are processed through secure payment providers. WaziTrade does not store your complete payment card information. Transactions are encrypted to protect your personal and financial information.

Third Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers for payment processing, delivery services, cloud hosting, analytics and customer support. These providers receive only the information necessary to perform their services.

Your Rights

Contact
